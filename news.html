Sunrisers Hyderabad Ready for IPL 2024

With new signings and a strong core, SRH looks set to make a deep run this season.

The 2024 season was the 12th season for the Indian Premier League franchise Sunrisers Hyderabad. They were one of the ten teams competed in the 2024 Indian Premier League. They finished at the last place in previous season's League stage.[1] Ahead of the 2024 season, Pat Cummins was appointed as the captain following a poor 2023 season led by Aiden Markram.[2] After their abandoned match on 16 May 2024, Hyderabad qualified for the Playoffs.[3][4] They finished the League stage at the 2nd place with 8 wins and 5 losses, garnering 17 points and seeding their place in the Qualifier 1.[5] After being defeated by Kolkata Knight Riders in the Qualifier 1 played on 21st May at Ahmedabad, Hyderabad advanced to the Qualifier 2.[6][7] After defeating Rajasthan Royals in the Qualifier 2 played on 24th May at Chennai, Hyderabad advanced to the 2024 final for the 3rd time.[8][9] After being defeated by Kolkata Knight Riders in the final played on 26th May at Chennai, Hyderabad finished as the runners-up for the 2nd time after 2018.[10][11] Nitish Kumar Reddy was named as the "Emerging player of the season" and was awarded with ₹10 lakh (US$13,000) cash prize. Abhishek Sharma hit most sixes in the season (42)[12] while Travis Head hit most fours in the season (64)[13] and both were awarded with ₹10 lakh (US$13,000) cash prize and a trophy to each. Sunrisers Hyderabad won the "Team fairplay award" of ₹10 lakh (US$13,000) cash prize.[14]

***************All records broken and new records set by SRH***************

Sunrisers Hyderabad broke their own record (277) for the highest innings total in IPL (287). Royal Challengers Bengaluru broke Mumbai Indians' record (246) for the highest second innings total in IPL (262), and South Africa's record (259) for the highest second innings total in T20 cricket.

The Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) broke several records in the 2024 Indian Premier League (IPL), including: Highest PowerPlay score in T20 cricket: SRH scored 125/0 against the Delhi Capitals (DC) Fastest fifty: Travis Head scored a fifty in 16 balls, equaling the IPL 2024 record Highest run rate for a partnership: Head and Abhishek Sharma had a run rate of 20.68 for a partnership of more than 100 runs Most sixes in an IPL innings: SRH hit 22 sixes against DC, equaling their own record Highest IPL total: SRH scored 287/3 against the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB), which was also the highest total ever in IPL history Fastest team 100: SRH scored 100 runs in five overs against DC, the fastest in men's T250 history Fastest chase of a 150+ score: SRH chased down 166 runs against the Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) in 9.4 overs, with unbeaten half-centuries from both openers
